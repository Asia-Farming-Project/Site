Privacy Policy

This page outlines what data is collected, how it's used, and your control over it.

  • A small, closed group of players are given an application that runs alongside Dead by Daylight.
  • This app allows for role selection and queue time timers.
  • When a match begins, a timestamp fragment is sent to a private server to record estimated queue time.
  • This raw data is stored securely for 15 days. After that period, an average is calculated from all entries - including time slots - and the raw data is deleted.
  • No data is collected from the general public. Only authorized users can run the tracking tool, and access is limited due to the system’s vulnerability to reverse engineering.
  • Aggregated/analyzed data (averages, trends) may be published publicly, but never includes user-specific information.
  • No data is stored or submitted without the explicit, end-to-end permission of the user involved.
  • If a user requests their dataset to be deleted, that request will be honored within 5 business days. Once removed, their data is also excluded from the latest average results.
